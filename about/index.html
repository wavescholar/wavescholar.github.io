Bruce B Campbell - Developer

I am a Data Science leader and individual contributor with lots of industry experience in machine learning, computer vision and imaging systems, NLP, and computational digital pathology

I went to the University of Rochester and obtained degrees in physics (BS) and Mathematics (MS). My interest was in mathematical physics. I was planning to go to work in industry after receiving my MS, but was enticed by an award and some funding to stay longer and work towards a PhD. My adviser Professor Martin Feinberg was in the Chemical Engineering department. We worked on qualitative models of stability in mass action chemical reaction networks. I was in the PhD program for two years when I left to pursue a career in software engineering.
